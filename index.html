James Hortman, founder of Kingdom Lens Productions.

Kingdom Lens wasn't built in a boardroom. It was built on a belief that every business has a story worth telling and that the right story, told the right way, changes everything.

Based in Atlanta, we work with local businesses who are serious about growth. Not hobbyists. Not dabblers. Business owners who understand that visibility is everything and are ready to invest in showing up like the leader they already are.

Our approach is simple: we don't just show up and shoot. We become a strategic content partner, understanding your goals, your audience, and your brand before a single camera comes out of the bag.
